OIL OF EVERY PEARL'S UN-INSIDES NON-STOP REMIX ALBUM

The OIL OF EVERY PEARL'S UN-INSIDES NON-STOP REMIX ALBUM is an album by Sophie of remixes and B-Sides from her album Oil Of Every Pearl's Un-Insides. It was released in the format of a Clutch Bag with 3 CDs on July 17 2019.

Background

Oil of Every Pearl's Un-Insides Non-Stop Remix Album (stylised in all caps) is the only remix album by Scottish recording artist and producer Sophie. It was the final full-length album released during the artist's lifetime. It features alternative versions and remixes of songs from Sophie's debut studio album Oil of Every Pearl's Un-Insides as well as new songs, presented as a non-stop DJ mix.

The project received very positive reviews, with critics praising its variety and dancefloor energy, highlighting its blend of techno, house, and ambient styles. Retrospective reviews also credited the album with setting a high bar for remix albums and showcasing Sophie's lasting influence.
